PRIVACY POLICY SUMMARY

AI Quick Help Extension Privacy Policy:

• DATA COLLECTION: We only process text that you manually select and choose to send to LLM services. No automatic data collection occurs.

• STORAGE: All settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage. We cannot access this data.

• PERMISSIONS: 
  - Storage: Save your preferences locally
  - Scripting: Inject UI elements into pages
  - Tabs: Interact with current tab
  - All URLs: Work on any website you choose

• SHARING: We do not share your data with third parties, except when you explicitly send text to your chosen LLM service.

• SECURITY: All data processing happens locally in your browser. API keys and settings remain on your device.

• YOUR CONTROL: You control what text to select, when to send it, and can clear all data by uninstalling the extension.
