Privacy Policy for AI Quick Help Extension

Last Updated: [Current Date]

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the AI Quick Help Chrome extension ("we," "our," or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Text Selections

The extension processes text that you manually select on web pages to send to Large Language Models (LLMs). This text is:

  • Only collected when you actively select it
  • Processed locally in your browser
  • Sent to your chosen LLM service only when you explicitly submit it

2.2 Settings and Preferences

We store your extension settings locally using Chrome's storage API, including:

  • LLM service preferences
  • API keys (stored locally on your device)
  • User interface preferences

2.3 Web Page Access

The extension requires broad web access permissions to function on any website you choose to use it on. We do not automatically collect or transmit any web page content without your explicit action.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Process and format text selections for LLM submission
  • Store your preferences and settings locally
  • Provide the core functionality of the extension

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage mechanisms. We do not have access to this data.

4.2 Third-Party Services

When you send text to an LLM service, that data is subject to the privacy policy of the respective LLM provider. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • When you explicitly send text to an LLM service of your choice
  • As required by law or to protect our rights

6. Permissions Explanation

Our extension requests the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your settings and preferences locally
  • Scripting: To inject our user interface elements into web pages
  • Tabs: To interact with the current active tab
  • Host Permissions (all URLs): To work on any website you choose to use the extension on

7. Data Retention

Settings are retained until you uninstall the extension or manually clear them. Text selections are not permanently stored and exist only temporarily during processing.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear all stored settings at any time
  • Uninstall the extension to remove all local data
  • Control what text you select and send to LLM services

9. Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the Chrome Web Store or extension updates.
